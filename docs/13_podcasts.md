# 🎙️ SWALPA Podcasts

Listen to deep dives into the linguistics, history, and survival tactics of Bangalore Kannada.

## Episode 1: Namma Ooru 101 - The Linguistic DNA of Bangalore Kannada

*Recorded: February 2026*

In this overview episode, we break down the historical layers of the Kannada language, from its Dravidian roots to Sanskrit and Perso-Arabic influences, and how to instantly recognize thousands of words.
