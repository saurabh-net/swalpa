# 🧠 Flashcards

> [!TIP] 🧠 **Master Kannada Faster with Spaced Repetition**
> We use [Orbit](https://withorbit.com/) to power these interactive flashcards. Orbit uses a **spaced repetition algorithm** that perfectly times when you see a card again to maximize your memory retention—helping you internalize these lessons permanently.
> 
> **Your progress is saved entirely on your device (in local storage).** You won't lose your progress if you refresh the page or come back tomorrow!

## Why Flashcards?

Flashcards are the "engine room" of language learning. They help you bridge the gap between understanding a rule and actually using a word in the heat of a conversation.

### Two-Way Training

| Direction | Why Use It? | Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **English → Kannada** | **Active Recall**: Forces your brain to retrieve the Kannada word from scratch. | **Speaking**: Essential for when you want to say something. |
| **Kannada → English** | **Passive Recognition**: Trains you to recognize words as you hear them on the street. | **Listening**: Essential for understanding what people say to you. |
